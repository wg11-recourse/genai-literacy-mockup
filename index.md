This is a short, self-paced introduction to working with generative AI as a computer scientist. It goes past "what is a chatbot" to the things that matter when you build software: how these models actually produce output, how to judge code they generate, and where leaning on them quietly costs you the fundamentals.

<box type="info" seamless>

**A living course.** This material is regenerated on a schedule. Generative AI moves fast — models, norms, and tooling shift in months — so the course is rebuilt to keep pace rather than left to go stale.

</box>
